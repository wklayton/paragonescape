Return of the Pharaohs

Book Now

Overview

You are the ancient souls of the Egyptian pharaohs, whose afterlife abilities were bound by Set, the god of chaos.

Your only ally is the sun god, Ra. Alas, Ra’s powers have become limited due to the dwindling of sacrifices and offerings dedicated to him over the centuries.

In a final effort to restore balance to the heavens, Ra stripped himself of his last remaining magic and bestowed it upon you, turning you mortal one last time.

However, magic comes with a price.

You have one hour of mortality to enter the ancient pyramid of Shabaka, unravel the gods’ protective enchantments, overcome Set’s curse, and restore Ra’s full power once again.

If you succeed, Ra will grant that you remain to rule over Egypt in glory once more.

Remember, a single hour will decide your fate as the almighty pharaohs of Egypt, to reign united or be cursed.

Forgotten for all time.
